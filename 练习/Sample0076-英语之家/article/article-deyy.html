最好的学习团队 The Best Study Team

 相关标签

作者:chloe 来源:互联网 时间:2017-12-29
  • 学习（208）
  • 小美文
 

In the English class, the teacher asked us to set up the study group, so that she could give us some interesting tasks. There were four students in each team. At first, my team did not operate well, because we hadn’t got familiar with each other. But as the time went by, we started to adjust our methods and the way we worked was so well. Now, we helped each other to improve our English, the tasks that given by the teacher were so easy for us. It is the best study team for me. I learn a lot and build strong relationship with my friends. The team will exist all the time.

在英语课上，老师让我们成立了学习小组，这样她可以给我们一些有趣的任务。每个小组有四个学生。起初，我的团队没有配合好，因为我们对彼此都不熟悉。但随着时间的流逝，我们开始调整方法，我们的新方式起作用了。现在，我们互相帮助来提高我们的英语水平。老师给出的任务对我们来说也变得简单了。这对我来说是最好的学习团队。我学到了很多东西。我和我的朋友们也建立了稳固的关系。这个团队会一直存在。
